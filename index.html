Goal

Be the first player to reach 100 points.

Gameplay

On a turn, a player rolls the die repeatedly until either:

  • A 1 is rolled
  • The player chooses to hold (stop rolling)

If a 1 is rolled, that player's turn ends and no points are earned. If the player chooses to hold, all of the points rolled during that turn are added to his or her score
